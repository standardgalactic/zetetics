Erratum 
Erratum to “A Survey of U.S. Audiologists’ Usage of 
and Attitudes Toward Otoacoustic Emissions” 
Title: A Survey of U.S. Audiologists’ Usage of and Attitudes Toward Otoacoustic Emissions 
Authors: Ian B. Mertes and Ali Marquess 
American Journal of Audiology (2023), 32(2), 417–431 
https://doi.org/10.1044/2023_AJA-22-00096 
In the original article, the authors incorrectly described the group of colleagues who provided feedback on a revised 
draft of the survey as a “focus group.” Each colleague received an electronic copy of the draft survey and independently 
provided written feedback to the authors. The authors apologize for this error.
