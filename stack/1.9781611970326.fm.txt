Acknowledgments
These lectures were given at the CBMS—NSF Regional Conference held in
Lowell, Massachusetts, March 19-23, 1979. I would like to thank Gerald Kaiser
for arranging the conference and making the stay in Lowell and Nashua pleasant
for all the participants. Some of the lectures of the other participants and solicited
papers appear in the Springer Lecture Note Series (#775). I thank all the
participants for their helpful comments and lecture notes. This includes especially
Boris Kuperschmidt, Vincent Moncrief and Tudor Ratiu for help with the lecture
notes, and Stuart Antman, Stanley Deser, Gerard Emch, Mark Gotay, Morton
Gurtin, Robert Hermann, Gerald Kaiser, André Lichnerowicz, Vincent
Moncrief, Jim Nester, and Wlodek Tulczyjew for their comments and lectures.
Other colleagues and collaborators with whom parts of this work were done
jointly are Judy Arms, Paul Chernoff, David Chillingworth, Arthur Fischer, Philip
Holmes, Tom Hughes, Vincent Moncrief, Tudor Ratiu, Steve Wan and Alan
Weinstein.
Finally, thanks are due to Connie Calica and Marnie McElhiney for a fine job of
typing the manuscript.
Research contained in this monograph was partially supported by the National
Science Foundation under grant MCS 78-06718, and by the U.S. Army Research
Office under contract DAAG 29-79C-0086.
