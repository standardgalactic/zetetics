Research Interests 
 
Cognition and cognitive neuroscience. Comprehension and memory; reality monitoring processes 
(e.g., cues used to distinguish real from imagined events) and source monitoring in general; 
autobiographical memory; component processes of cognitive subsystems; consciousness; executive 
processes; the self; disrupted cognition; emotion and cognition; aging and memory 
 
Teaching Interests 
 
Component processes of cognition and memory; psychological and social/cultural processes of 
reality monitoring; special topics seminars (e.g., selected topics in cognition and cognitive 
neuroscience; amnesia; consciousness; autobiographical memory; concepts of volition, agency, 
responsibility and control; topics in law and psychology)
